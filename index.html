About

AI For Good @ SUSS is a forward-looking initiative aimed at harnessing applied AI, LLM, GPT, and various other tools to enhance education. We firmly believe that AI has the potential to elevate learning, teaching, assessment, and all facets of the SUSS academic ecosystem. Our core objective is to leverage AI in pursuit of our social mission: inspiring lifelong learning and catalyzing societal transformation through applied AI.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines, encompassing a wide range of technologies and applications that enable computers to perform tasks such as problem-solving, decision-making, and learning.

Large Language Model

LLMs are advanced AI systems that generate human-like text, trained on vast data, enabling efficient communication, comprehension, and creativity with diverse applications.

Generative Pre-trained Transformers

GPT are deep learning language models, pre-trained on extensive text corpora, are capable of generating coherent, contextually relevant text. These models have found applications in areas like text generation and language understanding.
